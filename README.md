# StartStack

> **Building the one-stop legal operating system for startups and SMBs — from incorporation to contracts, compliance, and global expansion.**

Startups and small businesses shouldn't need a $500/hr lawyer for every legal decision they face. StartStack is an AI-powered legal platform that handles the full lifecycle of a company's legal needs — starting with the very first decision every founder has to make: how to incorporate.

---

## Company Vision

Legal infrastructure for businesses is broken. It's expensive, inaccessible, and built for big companies with in-house counsel. StartStack is building the legal OS for the other 99% — solo founders, co-founder teams, and small businesses who need real legal help without the billable hours.

**One platform. Every legal need a startup has.**

---

## The 5 Modules

### 1. Incorporation
*From "what do I even form?" to incorporated — in minutes.*
- Guided wizard → AI recommends the right entity type and state
- Plain-English explanation of the decision
- Generates and submits incorporation filings on your behalf

### 2. Service & Employment Agreements
*Answer questions. Get a legally sound draft in seconds.*
- Service agreements for contractors and vendors
- Employment agreements for full-time hires
- AI asks the right questions, drafts the document, explains every clause

### 3. Patents & IP Protection
*Don't lose your idea to someone else.*
- Understand what can be patented
- Step-by-step guidance through the IP protection process
- Prior art search guidance, provisional patent prep

### 4. Fundraising
*Know your terms before you sign them.*
- Generate term sheets for pre-seed and seed rounds
- Understand SAFEs, convertible notes, and priced rounds
- AI explains what every clause means for founder dilution and control
